# PourChoices

Your worst nights make the best data.

## What it is
Strava for your bar crawl. PourChoices logs your BAC splits, maps your route between bars, and tracks nic hits (zyns, cigs) along the way. At the end of the night you get a full breakdown of where you went, how long you stayed, and how cooked you got ready to share (or hide) from your friends.

## Core Features
- BAC tracking with splits per bar
- Live map of your crawl route
- Nicotine logging (zyns, cigs)
- Location-based auto-prompts when you leave a venue
- Post-night summary with stats and route replay
- See your friends stats and compare nights...

## How it works
1. Start a session
2. PourChoices detects when you arrive at and leave a bar
3. Log your drinks (and zyns) at each stop
4. App calculates estimated BAC splits in real time
5. End the session and review your night
